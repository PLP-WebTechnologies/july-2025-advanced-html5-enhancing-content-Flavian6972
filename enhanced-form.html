Gross Anatomy Revision Bootcamp

About the Bootcamp

Gross Anatomy Revision Bootcamp is a free, two-day high-yield Session tailored for first year medical students.
In this Session, we will cover key anatomical regions, practise with real-time specimen and explore clinical correlations to help you master the essentials and feel exam-ready

Topics to be Covered

  • Lower Limb
  • Upper Limb
